Agility, Speed, and Strength are all traits you need to acquire to perform to you full ability in CISS volleyball. The Varsity girls got 2nd place at APAC and first at SISAC. The Varsity guys got 6th place at APAC and 3rd place at SISAC. The Junior Varsity girls got 1st place at SISAC and the guys got 5th place at SISAC.


For those of you that don’t know what APAC (Asia Pacific Activities Conference), its when everyone in the conference meet up somewhere in Asia to challenge each other at one school to become that years champions! SISAC is where all the schools in Shanghai and out of Shanghai come and meet up at a school in Shanghai to battle each other to become that years champions.


These are both great events to attend and have fun at.
